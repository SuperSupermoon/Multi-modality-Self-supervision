Radiodense tip of intra-aortic balloon pump terminates approximately 2.4 cm below the superior aspect of the aortic knob. Cardiomegaly is accompanied by mild pulmonary vascular congestion and a worsening nonspecific opacity in the periphery of the right mid lung which may reflect focal atelectasis or potentially a developing pneumonia. Short-term followup radiographs may be helpful in this regard if warranted clinically.
