GREATER OPACIFICATION BASE OF THE LEFT HEMI THORAX IS DUE IN PART TO INCREASED SMALL LEFT PLEURAL EFFUSION. THERE MAY ALSO BE PERIBRONCHIAL OPACIFICATION IN BOTH LOWER LUNGS, CONCERNING FOR ASPIRATION OR AT LEAST ATELECTASIS. MODERATE CARDIOMEGALY IS CHRONIC, BUT THERE IS NO PULMONARY VASCULAR OR MEDIASTINAL VENOUS DILATATION TO SUGGEST CARDIAC DECOMPENSATION.
