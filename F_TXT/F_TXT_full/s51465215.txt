IN COMPARISON WITH THE EARLIER STUDY OF THIS DATE, THERE HAS BEEN PLACEMENT OF A OROGASTRIC TUBE THAT EXTENDS TO THE DISTAL STOMACH. BIBASILAR OPACIFICATION PERSISTS, CONSISTENT WITH A COMBINATION OF VOLUME LOSS IN THE LOWER LOBES AND PLEURAL EFFUSION. IN THE APPROPRIATE CLINICAL SETTING, SUPERIMPOSED PNEUMONIA WOULD HAVE TO BE CONSIDERED.
