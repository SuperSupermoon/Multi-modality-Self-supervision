It is very difficult to distinguish colonic interposition in the right upper abdominal quadrant from multi loculated pneumo peritoneum. That determination is more reliably made with dedicated abdominal imaging. The right pleural effusion however is the most likely reflective of the abdominal contents, whether ascites, infection, or bile leak.
